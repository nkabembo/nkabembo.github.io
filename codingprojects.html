Flexible Work Location Tool

About the Flexible Work Location Tool

Flexible Work Location Tool is a capstone project made for Interior Health at Okanagan College. It's purpose is to streamline requests to work from home , to help the company abide to governmental regulations about working from home and to get rid of form reviewing via the sending of pdfs by email. It includes the submissions of forms, the receiving and reviewing of forms and many other components. The application used a data driven approach in the way it handles forms and is programmed using C#

My contributions

I contributed to the project in my role as a UX designer as well as a programmer. In terms of programming I did some back end code but my main contribution I would say was in the front-end coding.
